Built for the way you work

Demos, recordings, presentations, debugging — all in a single, beautiful native app.

⚡

Zero latency

USB connection means real-time mirroring with sub-frame latency. No more laggy AirPlay.

📱

iPhone & iPad frames

Your device appears in an authentic bezel with Dynamic Island for modern iPhones and uniform bezels for iPads.

🎥

Screen recording

Record the mirrored stream straight to a .mov file in your chosen folder. Native H.264.

📸

Instant snapshots

Capture the current frame as a PNG with one click or ⌘S. Annotations are baked right into the export.

🔄

Live rotation

Rotate the device frame with ⌘← / ⌘→, or let it follow your iPad's physical orientation automatically.

🔍

Pinch to zoom

Zoom in up to 4× with a pinch, drag to pan around pixel-perfect details, ⌘0 to reset.

✏️

Annotation tools

Draw, highlight, and add arrows directly on the mirrored screen. Undo with ⌘Z, toggle with A.

🎨

Custom backgrounds

Choose from 7 built-in gradients or pick your own color. Make your demos and screenshots look stunning.

🖼️

Bezel styles

Switch between Classic, Floating, and Frameless display modes. Match your aesthetic for any context.

🖥️

Fullscreen mode

One click to expand into a stunning gradient background. Perfect for presentations.

📌

Always on top

Pin MirrorKit above every other window so your device stays visible while you work.

⌨️

Keyboard first

Every action has a shortcut — record, snapshot, rotate, toggle frame, zoom. Open the Capture menu to discover them.

🔒

Privacy first

Runs 100% locally. No analytics, no telemetry, no network calls. Ever.

🪶

Lightweight

Native Swift. Under 5 MB. Instant launch. Low CPU and battery usage.

🧩

Multi-device picker

Plug in several iPhones or iPads and switch between them from the floating toolbar in one click.
